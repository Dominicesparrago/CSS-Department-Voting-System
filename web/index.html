Built-in Integrity

Every vote protected

Anti-cheating is enforced by the system itself — not by trust.

1×

One vote per position

Each ballot record is immutable. Duplicate or after-close votes are rejected at the source.

YR

Year-scoped ballots

You can only vote for the Representative of your own year level — no cross-year ballots.

✓

All-at-once submission

Your full ballot is cast in a single atomic action — no partial or top-up votes later.

@

Verified registration

Strict .scc email format and a unique 7–9 digit student ID keep accounts genuine.

🔒

Secret ballot

No student can read another student's vote. Your choices stay private, always.

⤓

Auditable results

Final tallies are recomputed from immutable records, so outcomes are fully verifiable.
